Profile

Curator and conservator with fourteen years devoted to the preservation, interpretation, and exhibition of rare books, illuminated manuscripts, and early printed matter. I bring the scholarship of a bibliographer and the care of a craftsperson to collections that exist at the threshold of art, history, and fragile beauty.
